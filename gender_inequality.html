What is Gender Inequality?

Gender inequality refers to gender discrimination, meaning the ability to distinguish people based on their sex. This is a social problem that impacts education, employment, politics, healthcare, and all aspects of human social interaction.

Causes of Gender Inequality

  • Limited resource accessibility
  • Social and spiritual actions
  • Conflict and physical security issues
  • Economic dependency
  • Media representation

Created by: Demilade Banwo

Effects of Gender Inequality

Gender inequality negatively impacts societies by slowing economic development, increasing poverty levels, and perpetuating violence. Addressing these issues can foster fairness and equality.
